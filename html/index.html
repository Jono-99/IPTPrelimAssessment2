Weight Sensor

Ovis is able to track the weight of your luggage in real-time. This can simplify life beyond belief when you are packing so you don’t over-pack or worry about under-packing.

Removable Battery

With the press of one button, you can pop out the battery, go through security and then pop it in and continue enjoying your hands-free luggage.

Large Internal Storage

Ovis' large interior volume of 34L can easily hold enough items for a whole week trip. With durable yet lightweight material, Ovis makes your travel lite and smart.hat does really great things.

Easily Switch to Manual Mode

By simply grabbing the handle, you cut all motor power and engage manual mode. This implies that you can drag the luggage at your wish.

Full Featured App

Make it simple! With the customized Ovis App, you’ll be able to track your Ovis, measure the weight of your belongings, monitor your Ovis’ battery life, all with just one click.
